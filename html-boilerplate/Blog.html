Why im coding.

Growing up, language arts was never my favorite class. I'd sit through BORING lectures wondering why I needed to learn a language I already knew. But as I completed class after class, I came to the realization of what it was all for.

Being able to express ones ideas clearly is paramount to success in life, and as I grow older, more and more I need to express that beyond just writing.

Learning full stack development will enable me to not only express my ideas and motivations in writing, but also in a medium that lets me express my professional skills.
